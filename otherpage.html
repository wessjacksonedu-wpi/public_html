About Oranges

Oranges are one of the most popular fruits worldwide! Packed with vitamin C and a refreshing flavor, they're not just delicious, they're nutritious too!

Oranges are a hybrid of pomelos and mandarins.

There are over 600 varieties of oranges, including sweet, bitter, and navel oranges.

Oranges are rich in vitamin C, helping to boost your immune system.

The largest producer of oranges in the world is Brazil, followed by the United States.

An average orange contains about 70-85% water, making it a great way to stay hydrated.

Oranges have been cultivated for over 4,000 years, dating back to ancient China.

In addition to vitamin C, oranges are a good source of fiber, potassium, and folate.

Oranges are often used in marmalade, juices, and desserts, and can also be enjoyed fresh!
